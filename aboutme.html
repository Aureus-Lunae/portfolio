About Me

I am a developer, poet, speedrun journalist and speedrunner in the Netherlands. As a poet, I write when I have inspiration. As a speedrunner, I run games whenever I have time. And as a journalist, I write articles for Indie Game Press.

Writing and Reading
Inspiration

There is something we want
As we wander through the depths of darkness
Searching for an idea
Not knowing where to start
We descend in the thoughts
First nothing
Chaos
Then a stroke of inspiration
We try to remember it
As we grab the paper
When the pen comes down
The first word written
It is gone
Forever lost

Curriculum Vitae
Speedrunner
Beating games as fast as possible

Speedrunning is the act of beating games as fast as possible. It is a constant attempt to improve your times. I myself prefer to run Sega Genesis/Mega Drive games. Sundays at 19:00 CET there are races I join if the games are interresting.
